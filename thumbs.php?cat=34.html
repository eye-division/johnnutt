travellers

Moved from London to the West Country where I now work as a writer and artist, on the Blackdown Hills. Retired from art education in 2007 but do occasional consultations.
 
First art degree in sculpture at the West of England Academy and then Art education at London University Institute of Education. After a long career as head of Arts Faculty in Redbridge, I retired after several years of training art teachers. Went on to create digital art education websites and work as an art ed consultant for RM Cambridge, the Bridgeman Art Library, and others. My artwork now engages with more meaningful content. Recent paintings are concerned with narrative and the recession.
