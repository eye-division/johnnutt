canal nymph
trouble at mill
Fanny Mae

Artwork 2014

Recently been working on paintings that  tell a story with contrasting elements. I am interested in developing the psychological aspects of a complex image and telling a story that the viewer can interpret as they wish. The paintings are intended to reveal their meaning slowly.
