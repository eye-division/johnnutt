Cutsey House and Brendon Hills
Quantock 4
Quantock End

Landscape painting. 

These paintings are landscapes of Somerset and Devon that depict a distant view of  the Blackdown Hills, Brendon Hills or the Quantocks.  They are about how we see space, but the scenes that I work from are often complex and the painting is the result of drawing and editing the image repeatedly.  The paintings are made with the simplest and the most accurate marks, developing the configuration with drawing and careful observation.
